Inside the Book...

A glimpse into the adventures of Xie Lian and Hua Cheng

Eight hundred years after his fall from grace, Xie Lian,a once the beloved Crown Prince of Xianle ascends to the heavens for the third time. Stripped of power and followers, he becomes the subject of ridicule among gods. Tasked with investigating strange ghostly affairs on earth, he encounters San Lang, a mysterious young man whose charm and devotion hide a darker truth. Together, they uncover the tale of a tragic ghost bride, marking the beginning of Xie Lian’s return to a world filled with both divine duty and haunting memory.

Having formed an unlikely partnership with San Lang, Xie Lian begins to take on new heavenly missions that lead him deep into the realm of restless spirits. Each encounter reveals more about the boundary between gods and ghosts and the compassion that can exist even in death. As Xie Lian learns more about San Lang’s true nature, he starts to question what truly separates the living from the damned, and whether redemption is ever truly out of reach.

The mysteries of the past begin to resurface as Xie Lian faces remnants of his own kingdom’s downfall. Hidden truths about the Four Great Calamities, powerful ghosts who defied the heavens, come to light, and their connection to Xie Lian’s story grows undeniable. As he navigates deception and divine hypocrisy, he must confront both the sins of gods and his own lingering guilt. Amid the chaos, the quiet devotion between him and Hua Cheng becomes a light that cuts through centuries of darkness.

Tensions rise between the heavens and the ghost realms, revealing the fragile order that holds the three worlds together. Old allies become strangers, and former enemies show unexpected kindness. Xie Lian is forced to see the cruelty hidden behind divine glory and the loyalty buried within those cast aside. His past, once shrouded in tragedy, now reveals its sharpest edges where love and ruin were born from the same act of faith.

As long-buried histories come to the surface, the fates of gods, ghosts, and mortals intertwine. Xie Lian’s path collides with those of other fallen deities, each with their own story of despair and defiance. The truth behind his multiple ascensions begins to unfold, showing how mercy and misfortune have always walked hand in hand. Every choice he’s made leads him closer to understanding Hua Cheng’s unwavering devotion and the cost of such eternal love.

With all secrets laid bare, Xie Lian must face the consequences of centuries-old actions and divine manipulation. Betrayals strike from unexpected places, and the truth threatens to tear apart what little peace remains in the heavens. Through pain, forgiveness, and revelation, Xie Lian learns what it truly means to be a god not through power, but through compassion. The bond between him and Hua Cheng is tested, yet it becomes more unbreakable than ever.

War looms across the realms, and the fragile line between salvation and destruction begins to blur. Xie Lian stands at the center of a storm that will decide the future of gods and ghosts alike. In the chaos, old wounds resurface, and every sacrifice demands a reckoning. The choices he makes now will either bring peace to a world drenched in sorrow or plunge it into eternal ruin.

After centuries of turmoil, the story comes full circle. The heavens find balance once again, and those who suffered finally find rest. For Xie Lian and Hua Cheng, their journey — filled with laughter, tears, and redemption — reaches a quiet, tender conclusion. Love that endured death, time, and divinity becomes something eternal, something that even the heavens themselves must bless.
